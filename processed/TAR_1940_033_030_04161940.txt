Arrangements Made lor Use 
Of Mechanical Itallol 'Boxes
For many years the charge of stuffing the ballot boxes 
has been made against those in control of elections. Last year 
a survey showed that in a case where all but 27 Juniors were 
supposed to have voted, at least 40 names of non-voting' Ju
niors were collected.
To correct this abuse the Tartan has secured the coopera
tion of the Elections Head of Allegheny County who will, free 
of any charge, place mechanical ballot boxes on the campus for 
the important April 30th election.
The University of Pittsburgh used mechanical ballot boxes 
in its last election and they proved a tremendous success. In 
an interview with the Tartan editor, Mr. Robert Corley, As
sistant Dean of Men at Pitt, said: "The machines are fool
proof, simple to operate, and stimulate student interest in elec
tions. In addition, they provide excellent citizenship training. 
Tabulation of results is also facilitated since the machines total 
the results automatically.
Mr. David Olbum, Elections Head of Allegheny County, is 
now waiting word from the Tartan and the Stiident Council 
Elections Committee to simply order the machines and have 
them set up on the campus in time for the next election. A 
few sample machines could be placed here in advance for dem
onstration purposes.
Can there be any objections to as desirable a plan as this?

VARSITY C CLUB?
 By DOC BURKE, Sports Editor 
Putting one word after anotherwhatever happened to 
the Varsity C ClubV

Organized, the idea was received with enthusiasm. Unfortunate
lyleast in this case, more than enthusiasm is needed to'get 
tilings going. At the first meeting called there was a very 
respectable turnout of captains and managers of the various 
athletic teams. But since that meeting not much lias been 
done.
In the organization of any such group there must be a few 
persons-who will do most of the work. Apparently no one wishes 
to take the initiative to follow through the plans which have 
been outlined. Possibly in the future somebody will step up
