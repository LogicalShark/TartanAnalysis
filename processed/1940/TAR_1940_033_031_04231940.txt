Now that Duquesne University lias a baseball team, Car
negie Tech remains the only school in the city that does not have 
a representative 011 the diamond. This is the Dukes first try 
with a nine sponsored by the school, but a team composed of 
students has played a schedule for a number of years. This 
seems like a good way to work into a regular school team; for if 
the athletic office is shown that a baseball team will stand up for 
a few consecutive years, there is a strong possibility that the 
team will be taken into the sports program of the school.
Pitt approached the situation a little differently, starting 
right' out in competition. For the past two years the Panthers 
have played an extensive schedule, making a trip through the 
South playing some of the best teams in that district.
For the past four years, at least, there have been fellows 
on the campus who have been interested in having a team. Two 
years ago a team was organized, but last year it was not con
tinued. Again this year a number of fellows are interested in 
having a team. But because of the weather it has been impos
sible for anything definite to be done about it. Therefore, the 
best thing for these fellows to do is to organize an independent 
team and play games as they can schedule them. If this move is 
made this year, possibly next year the same thing can be done. 
In this way sometime in the future Carnegie Tech may have a 
nine which will be included in the sports curriculum of the 
school.
